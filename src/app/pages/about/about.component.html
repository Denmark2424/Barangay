About The System

Understanding the Barangay Immunization Notification System

How It Works

1. Request Schedule

Parents submit a quick online form to register their baby's details and request an immunization schedule at a preferred clinic.

2. Review & Approve

Barangay health workers review incoming requests, confirm vaccine availability, and secure your schedule in the system.

3. Get Notified

You receive timely updates and reminders about appointments so your child never misses a crucial vaccine.

How It Helps Patients

⏱️

Convenience

Book schedules right from home without waiting in long, tedious queues at the health center.

📅

Timely Reminders

Automated notifications help you easily keep track of your baby's vaccination calendar.

🛡️

Assured Availability

Better tracking means health workers ensure the right vaccines are reserved for you prior to your visit.

📱

Accessible Management

Your requests and records are digitally organized, avoiding missing paper forms.
